# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2, 2026

Bottlr ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Bottlr mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide

- **Account Information**: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email and display name for authentication purposes.
- **Bottle & Cocktail Data**: Information you enter about your liquor collection, including names, distilleries, regions, notes, ratings, and other details.
- **Photos**: Images you take or select for your bottle and cocktail entries.

### Data Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: We may collect anonymous analytics data through Firebase Analytics to improve the App.

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide and maintain the App's functionality
- To sync your collection data across devices (when signed in)
- To improve and optimize the App experience

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted database.
- **Cloud Storage**: If you sign in with Google, your data is synced to Firebase (Google Cloud) servers to enable backup and cross-device access.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Firebase Authentication**: For Google Sign-In
- **Firebase Firestore**: For cloud data storage
- **Firebase Cloud Storage**: For photo backup
- **Firebase Analytics**: For anonymous usage analytics

These services are operated by Google and are subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Camera Permission

The App requests camera access solely to allow you to take photos of your bottles and cocktails. Photos are stored locally on your device and, if you choose to sync, in your private Firebase cloud storage. We do not access your camera for any other purpose.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your collection data is private and only accessible to you.

## Data Retention & Deletion

- Your data remains stored as long as you use the App.
- You can delete individual bottles or cocktails at any time within the App.
- To delete all cloud data, you can sign out and request deletion by contacting us.
- Uninstalling the App removes all local data from your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 21 years of age (or the legal drinking age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
